### Capture tips
- Before recording, pick the scenario, then hit **Reset** so the guided run starts fresh at the default threshold (Consumer **70** · Enterprise **62**).
- Shot 8 (threshold) is the one that proves the math is real and interactive — give it the most room.
- For a silent/GIF version, shots 4–6 (the pod hand-offs) and 8 (threshold) tell the whole story.
- Disable OS notifications; full-screen the browser; hide the bookmarks bar.
